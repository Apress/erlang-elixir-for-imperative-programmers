

Brown Bread

3 cups Wholemeal Flour
1 cup Water
1 teaspoon Salt
2 teaspoons Brown Sugar
1 tablespoon Virgin Olive Oil
1 teaspoon Dried Yeast

(1) Put all ingredients into the bread machine
(2) Choose function 3 for Brown Bread
(3) Press Start-button
(4) When baking program is finished, get the ready bread from the bread machine
